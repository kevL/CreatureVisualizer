Creature Visualizer
NwN2 Electron toolset plugin.
2021 jan 13
2.3.6.3

Installation
------------
Close the NwN2 Toolset.

Copy CreatureVisualizer.dll to your

<install>\Neverwinter Nights 2\NWN2Toolset\Plugins

folder.

Open the toolset and go to View|Options|Security and change

AllowPlugins=Load all plugins

Close and reopen the toolset.

Go to the Plugins menu and click Creature Visualizer. Select a creature
blueprint or an instanced creature in an open area and it should appear in the
visualizer.
